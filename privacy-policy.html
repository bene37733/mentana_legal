Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Mentana is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health tracking application. IMPORTANT: This app handles sensitive mental health data. We take your privacy extremely seriously.

2. Information We Collect

• Personal Information: Name and email for account creation • Mental Health Data: Mood entries, sleep data, stress levels, social contact, nutrition, exercise, screen time, personal notes, and chat messages • Technical Data: Device information, usage analytics, and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

• Mood Analysis: AI-powered insights about your mental health patterns • Personalized Experience: Customized recommendations and support • Progress Tracking: Visualize your mental health journey • Chat Support: AI-powered mental health conversations

4. Data Storage & Security

• Firebase (Google Cloud): Encrypted storage of your data • OpenAI: Temporary processing for AI analysis • Local Device: Cached data for offline access • End-to-End Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest

5. Data Sharing

We DO NOT share your data with: • Third parties (except OpenAI for AI analysis) • Advertisers • Employers • Insurance companies • Family members (without explicit consent)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

• Access: Request a copy of all your data • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information • Erasure: Delete your account and all data • Portability: Export your data in readable format • Restriction: Limit how we process your data • Objection: Object to certain data processing

7. Children's Privacy

• Minimum Age: 13 years old • Parental Consent: Required for users under 18 • Special Protections: Enhanced privacy for minors

8. Crisis Situations

If you're in immediate danger, we may contact emergency services. Your safety takes priority over privacy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions: • Email: mentana.hi@outlook.com • Response Time: 48 hours For complaints: • EU Users: Contact your local data protection authority • US Users: Federal Trade Commission (FTC)
