‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI personalization work?
After completing 3 mood check-ins, Mentana's AI analyzes your unique patterns - including mood trends, sleep quality, stress levels, and more. It then creates customized 7-day courses specifically designed for your needs. The more you use Mentana, the better it understands you.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. Your mental health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your personal information to third parties. You own your data and can request deletion at any time. Mentana is GDPR compliant.
How much does Mentana cost?
Mentana offers a free trial so you can experience the full app. After the trial, a subscription is required to continue using all features. Pricing is kept as affordable as possible to make mental health support accessible to everyone. Subscription revenue helps cover AI and server costs.
Can Mentana replace therapy?
No. Mentana is designed to support your mental wellness journey and complement professional care, but it's not a replacement for therapy or medical treatment. For serious mental health concerns, please consult a licensed healthcare professional.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime through the App Store:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name ‚Üí Subscriptions
  3. Select Mentana
  4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Thank you for helping improve Mentana! Please email mentana.hi@outlook.com with:
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce (if possible)
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
How do Therapy Projects work?
Therapy Projects are structured, AI-guided journeys focused on specific challenges like anxiety, stress, or self-worth. You work through exercises and reflections at your own pace, with the AI adapting based on your responses and progress.
What's included for athletes and performers?
Mentana helps athletes with mental training, managing pre-competition anxiety, building mental resilience, improving focus, and tracking performance-related mood patterns. The AI can create courses specifically for athletic performance optimization.
Can I use Mentana offline?
Most features require an internet connection for AI functionality and data syncing. However, you can view your past entries and insights offline.
How do I export my data?
You can request a full export of your data by emailing mentana.hi@outlook.com. We'll send you a complete copy in a portable format within 30 days.
What devices does Mentana support?
Mentana is currently available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 15.0 or later. We're constantly working to improve compatibility and add new features.
How does streak tracking work?
Complete daily mood check-ins to build your streak. Each consecutive day adds to your streak count. Streaks help build healthy habits and keep you motivated on your mental health journey.
